Name Mismatch Problem

Most Common Issue
‚ñº

Name spelling differs across Aadhaar, PAN, Bank account? Here's how to fix it.

üîçWhy This Happens

Name mismatch occurs when your name is spelled differently in various documents. Common issues: middle name missing, surname first, initials vs full name.

‚ö° Quick Solution

Standardize to Aadhaar: It's easiest to update other documents to match your Aadhaar card name, as Aadhaar is considered the primary ID.

üìù Step-by-Step Fix

  • Decide standard name: Choose the version you want everywhere (usually Aadhaar name)
  • Update PAN card: Visit NSDL/UTIITSL website, fill correction form
  • Update bank account: Visit branch with Aadhaar and request name correction
  • Update other docs: Use corrected PAN/bank docs as proof for other changes
  • Keep proof: Save all acknowledgment receipts

üìÑ Documents Needed

  • Aadhaar card (for proof of correct name)
  • Current document needing correction
  • Gazette notification (for major name changes)
  • Marriage certificate (if name changed after marriage)

Processing Time: PAN correction: 15-20 days | Bank update: Immediate to 7 days

Address Proof Issues

Very Common
‚ñº

Moved to new house? Old address on documents? Learn how to update everywhere.

üîç The Problem

When you move to a new address, all your government documents need to be updated. Old addresses can cause application rejections.

‚úÖ Accepted Address Proofs

  • Aadhaar card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID
  • Ration card
  • Electricity/water/gas bill (less than 3 months old)
  • Bank statement (less than 3 months old)
  • Rent agreement (registered)

üìù Update Priority Order

  • Update Aadhaar first:Online via UIDAI website or visit Aadhaar center
  • Update bank account:Visit branch with new address proof
  • Update mobile number:Link with new address for OTPs
  • Update PAN:Use updated Aadhaar as proof
  • Update other docs:Use Aadhaar + utility bill as proof

üí° Pro Tips

  • Keep 3 copies of your utility bill
  • Update mobile number immediately for OTP services
  • Carry both old and new address proofs during transition
  • Download digital copies of all updated documents

Fees: Aadhaar update: ‚Çπ50 | PAN update: ‚Çπ110 | Bank: Free

Photo/Signature Rejection

Frequent Problem
‚ñº

Application rejected due to photo or signature issues? Follow these guidelines.

üîçCommon Rejection Reasons

  • Photo is too old (more than 6 months)
  • Wearing sunglasses or cap
  • Blurry or low-quality image
  • Background not plain white
  • Signature not matching across documents
  • Digital signature not clear

‚úÖ Perfect Photo Requirements

  • Size:35mm x 45mm (passport size)
  • Background: Plain white (no designs)
  • Face: Looking straight, neutral expression
  • Clarity: Sharp focus, well-lit
  • Clothing:Avoid white clothes, no uniform
  • Accessories: No glasses (unless daily use), no cap

‚úÖ Signature Guidelines

  • Sign with black/blue pen on white paper
  • Keep 1 inch space around signature
  • Sign within a 3cm x 1cm box
  • Use the same signature for all documents
  • Practice your signature before scanning

üí° Digital Tips

Scan Settings: 200 DPI minimum | Format: JPG/JPEG | Size: 10KB-100KB

Mobile Photo: Use good lighting, hold phone steady, ensure plain background

Where to Get: Visit any photo studio and mention "Aadhaar/PAN photo" - they know requirements.
